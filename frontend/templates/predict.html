For Best Results

Follow these guidelines to ensure accurate analysis

Good Lighting

Ensure the face is well-lit with natural or soft artificial light, avoiding harsh shadows.

Front-Facing Position

The face should be directly facing the camera with a neutral expression for optimal analysis.

Clear Image Quality

Use high-resolution images where facial features are clearly visible and in focus.

Eyes Visible

Ensure both eyes are open and clearly visible without obstructions like glasses or hair.
